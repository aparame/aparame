### 👨‍🔬 About Me
- 🔭 I am currently a PhD Candidate in the **Interdisciplinary Intelligent Research Laboratory** at **Clemson University** under Dr. Yue "Sophie" Wang.
- 🌱 My research interests lie in **neurosymbolic deep learning**, **formal verification**, and **controls for robotics and autonomous vehicle applications**.
- 🎓 I hold an M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Purdue University and a B.E. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Pune.
- 💬 Ask me about **robotics, formal verification, semantic 3D mapping, and autonomous navigation**.

### 💻 Pinned & Notable Projects
* 🤖 **[SEVIN_ICCPS_RE_Package](https://github.com/aparame/SEVIN_ICCPS_RE_Package)**: Variational Autoencoders for explainable latent space encoding and efficient formal verification of neural network controllers.
* 🚗 **[Faster_TLG-RN](https://github.com/aparame/Faster_TLG-RN)**: 2D controller synthesis combining Linear and Signal Temporal Logic specifications to guarantee safe and robust navigation for ground robots.
* 🗺️ **Semantic 3D Mapping**: Deployed on Husky robots using the Octomap library, fusing semantically segmented RGB data with LiDAR point clouds.
* 🏎️ **[AI RC Car](https://github.com/aparame/AI_RC_Car)**: Developed an autonomous lane-keeping and sign-detecting RC car using ResNet-based CNNs and OpenCV.
